“One Piece” Website, 2022, HTML/CSS/Figma

This project was a recreation of the Crunchyroll “One Piece” website. I chose “One Piece” because it is one of my favorite animes. I used Figma to design the banner, the layout, the play button, the copyright image and text, and the images. I then translated the mockup into HTML/CSS. The site provides an overview of the show as well as descriptions of individual episodes and movies. This can be found at Loverta Brown: One Piece Website.
