Postcard: The Great Wall of China, 2022, Adobe Illustrator

I used the Adobe Illustrator Pen tool to create this image. I added a header and subheader to clarify the subject matter but took care of the text not to distract from the imagery. I chose the Great Wall of China because it is a place I want to visit. I looked at different typefaces and fonts to see which would look best with the type of landscape I picked: this font evokes Chinese calligraphy.
