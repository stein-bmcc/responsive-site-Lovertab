Lock and Key Animation, 2022, Cinema 4D

This animation depicts the room in which a weeping angel is locked. The sword on fire I created represents the “key” for the angels to escape. I also created the rest of the room, including the table, and glass container. It was my first big assignment in Cinema 4D: it incorporates, animation, materials, and rendering.
